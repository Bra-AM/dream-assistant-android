Dream Assistant

Voice-first, on-device AI personalized for speech impairments.

Key features:

  • 🔒 Privacy-first & offline
  • 🗣️ Fine-tuned per user
  • 🖥️ Minimal hands-free UI
  • 📱 Runs entirely on your phone

Download APK & Models

After downloading, open the .apk on your Android device to install.
If prompted, enable “Install unknown apps” for your browser or file manager.
Once installed, launch Dream Assistant and grant Microphone & Storage permissions when requested.
